ENGLISH: Sonnets

Lost in Cupid's Web

Welcome to the exciting world of English, where students start on an enthralling journey of literary discovery and linguistic mastery. In this course, students will dig into the rich tapestry of literature strengthening critical thinking skills and honing communication talents. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to build a profound appreciation for many literary genres, develop critical abilities, and allow students to successfully express themselves.

By the end of this course, students will not only have a greater understanding for the power of language and literature, but they will also have the skills and confidence to navigate the world as successful communicators and critical thinkers. Join us on this literary journey, where every page is an opportunity for intellectual progress and self-expression.

FEATURED WORKS

Cupid's Realm where love resides;
Bewildered, I stand, lost in a sea of affection.
Confusion comes to light with great tension,
Love ebbs and flows from passioned tides.
Why can't we, for once, express what makes us glide?
Say what we feel and run after the direction,
Let our hearts connect to perfection.
Take a chance with me and stay by my side.

Transcending hearts, fading like footsteps in the sand.
I know you better than the moon, but you forgot my birthday’s in June.
Call me a hopeless romantic, clinging on to a single thread of hope.
My heart sewn on my sleeve, longing to understand.
Thought it was everlasting love; then I was alone in the moon.
Felt like an invisible person in your globe.

Your gaze transported me to a past life;
A glance, a touch, a walk through the park.
Across the multiverse, you were there through my strife;
Suddenly my life lit up and you were the spark.

Through my wonders, it felt lonely and cold.
Like dying embers and a withered daisy.
You warmed me up like sunshine's gold,
On a stressful day when I feel hazy.

Love is a choice one has to make,
But know that what I feel for you is true.
Even with imperfections, mistakes, and heartache,
The only person I would choose through thick and thin is you.

Every day, I find peace in your presence.
Like daylight’s luminescence.

A lone star on eternity’s shore, my heart laid bare.
The ocean drips from my orbs, in rhythm with the tide.
Embraces I offered, but you forever denied.
My love, why do you flee? Your love, now a rare affair.
To gaze or to avoid, in your company, uncertainty is my air.
Your captivating beauty, now veiled in shadow’s guide,
The sparkle in your eyes, like an echo, slowly died.
Your presence there, a memory that once used to care.

Like a sunset’s farewell, you slipped from my embrace.
Like a candle’s flame extinguished, your light swiftly vanished.
Yet in my night of gloom, your name echoes with grace.
With a shattered heart, your love weaves a decent lace.
Beneath the moon's cool caress, your comfort I still long wished,
Choosing you through the pain until Venus' last kiss in space.

In chambers veiled, your tales remain unsaid.
Its key suspended aloft, a height too steep at its peak.
Could it, just once, gently tread right over my head,
Revealing all the answers to the wonders I long seek?

I bared my soul, yet your gaze craves our distant stroll.
Grant me empathy, for I tire of your hesitant fire.
In every corner of your cave, all I find is useless coal,
But for you, I will forever search for the rubies you desire.

In "Please wait a while," I’ll still hold you tight like rubber,
Yet you stab your evident lies, leaving my heart bleeding.
Darling, the sun's morning rays are too bright this summer
For me to lose you and call this our ending.

Broken promises believed, a fervent trust amassed,
Loved you above all, now yearn to break free at last.

Amidst the era of luminescent dreams, fate wove our way
Crafting the culmination of the existence of man
Yet Cupid's arrow wandered astray, fate's unpredictable span
In the ephemeral waltz of time, challenges sealed us away
My only act is to ponder if you're lost in a somber ballet
In fate's embroidery, reunion was grandiose plan
Dancing in the echoes of treasured hours, where it all began
Yet, no celestial grace stood witness, no predestined array

Adversities painted a void, unfathomable and unseen
Murdering the symphony of our once-vibrant love
Consuming fragments from our hearts, a sinister threat
Breaking fate's scheme, the desire to restore what has been
To mend the fractures, reclaiming our merited love
Hold on, we were just 2 dumb kids, how did we forget?

How do we dally in this fabric of togetherness?
Though our trust withdrawing like a sigh
A gloomy façade masking your genuineness
Mystery hiding your face, like clouds covering the sky

Doubt spins: was it the timing or the wrong place?
I bestowed upon you the entirety of my hearts
Your absence of such whispers disgrace
The reason that we may find ourselves drifting apart

Doubt, the singularity that devours our love
Fantasized about your occasional appearance
Please don't leave me, my one and only love
Though the odds favor your disappearance

In the closing chapter, we may find ourselves alone
We'd never last like cheap cologne
